Game overview:

A simple, beginner-made 2D game.

You are in a dark labyrinth, full to the brim of light-sensitive little freaks! You must reach the end while eliminating or avoiding these little freaks, with your only weapon being a... lantern... on a chain... [pause here, who made this up? Oh right, me]

Spin the lantern to make your light bigger! And hit the little freaks with it!

Beware the chatbox who gives the worst advice possble...

Controls: (so you actually play and not feel lost at the start)

-A = Go left

-D = Go right

-Space = Jump

-Left Mouse Button (Hold) = Spin the lantern

BTW, you need to spin the lantern to actually hit the little freaks! Not use it like a flashlight.
